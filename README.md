# Fabrication of Power Generating Shock Absorber

## Overview
This project demonstrates a sustainable energy recovery mechanism using vehicle shock absorbers.  
By converting mechanical vibrations into usable electrical energy, the prototype highlights innovation in renewable energy integration.

## Objectives
- Design and fabricate a shock absorber capable of energy harvesting.
- Integrate mechanical, pneumatic, and electrical subsystems.
- Validate energy recovery through testing and simulation.

## Tools & Technologies
- **Mechanical Design**: Fusion 360, AutoCAD
- **Simulation**: Ansys Workbench
- **Programming**: Python & C for data acquisition and efficiency calculations
- **Hardware**: Sensors, microcontrollers, fabricated prototype

## Results
- Prototype demonstrated measurable energy recovery during vibration cycles.
- Simulation validated efficiency and optimization areas.

## Future Scope
- Improve efficiency with advanced damping materials.
- Scale system for commercial vehicles.
- Integrate IoT-based monitoring for smart energy management.
